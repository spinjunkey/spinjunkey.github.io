Intel Level Admin

This will be the HR representative. Only and HR Rep can be at this level. This person has access to data at the corporate level down, i.e. Intel Corp and below.

Supergroups

- Update local db with employee data export from HR system.

- Assign super group admins

2

Supergroup Admin

This is the admin at the highest group level, i.e., TMG, DCG, IT, CCG, etc.

Business Groups

- Assign business group admins

3

Business Group Admin

This admin is at a business group level. If DCG is the supergroup, the business groups would be CG, DEG, DSG, IPAG, DSD, etc.

N/A

- Assigns the required and visible fields for the Manager

- Can see the employee data for the business group, as well as export at that level

4

Manager

This is the person at the lowest level that has employees. If DCG is our supergroup, this manager would reside in the business group such as CG.

N/A

- Edit and update data for that managers employees

- Export data for employees that report to this manager
