Creative Catalyst & Storyteller

 

Hi, I'm David — I’ve been an artist since childhood and am on a mission to help others see things "differently" through the power of creativity, play, and storytelling.

I love to observe people and the world around me to connect others to ideas and data that guides them down their path. I want to inspire others to act, change, and develop in new ways.

As a multi-skilled generalist – a multipotentialite – I look at the world with a global, holistic view. My curiosity allows me to view diverse perspectives. I prefer to leverage my understanding of human nature to solve problems through unique and unexpected approaches.

In addition, I’m a translator of languages and ideas, with the ability to simplify the complex while allowing others to understand difficult subjects.

Over my career, I have worked across multiple business functions including R&D, Product, IT, Operations, and HR. I suppose you can say it was my personalized MBA.

Please take a look at my portfolio below to see where we can collaborate together.
